Privacy Policy

FP SOCIAL  ·  ABN 29 393 516 521

Last updated: 23 June 2026

FP SOCIAL (ABN 29 393 516 521) ("FP SOCIAL", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in connection with our website at community.fpsales.com.au (the "Website") and the FP Elite community we operate.

We handle personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. About this policy and an important note on Circle

Our Website is primarily a gateway to our paid community. When you choose to join, you are redirected to a checkout and community platform operated by Circle (Circle.so), a third-party service based in the United States.

This means that most of the personal information involved in joining and using the community — including your name, email address, payment details, account login, profile information and anything you post — is collected and stored by Circle, not by us through this Website. Circle handles that information under its own privacy policy and terms, which we encourage you to read:

Circle Privacy Policy: circle.so/privacy-policy
Circle Terms of Service: circle.so/terms-of-service

This Privacy Policy covers how FP SOCIAL handles personal information. It does not cover how Circle, Meta, or any other third party independently handles your information — those parties are governed by their own policies.

2. What personal information we collect

Through this Website directly, we collect very little. Our Website does not have contact forms, signup boxes or payment fields — joining happens on Circle. The main information collected on our own Website is technical and usage data gathered automatically through cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 4), which may include:

  • your device type, browser and operating system;
  • your IP address and general location;
  • pages you viewed and how you interacted with the Website; and
  • referral information (the link or ad that brought you here).

Through Circle, when you join the community, Circle collects information such as your name, email address, payment information (processed by Circle's payment provider — we do not see or store full card details), profile details, and content you post in the community. We may access some of this information in our capacity as the community operator (for example, member names, email addresses and community activity).

3. How we use personal information

We use personal information to:

  • operate, promote and improve the Website and the FP Elite community;
  • understand how visitors find and use our Website, and measure the performance of our advertising;
  • communicate with members and respond to enquiries sent to us; and
  • comply with our legal obligations.

4. Cookies, analytics and the Meta (Facebook) Pixel

Our Website may use cookies and similar technologies. We may use the Meta Pixel (provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.) to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and to show relevant ads to people who have visited our Website. The Meta Pixel may collect information such as your IP address, device and browser information, and your activity on our Website, and shares this with Meta.

Information collected via the Meta Pixel is also subject to Meta's Privacy Policy (facebook.com/privacy/policy) and Meta's Cookie Policy (facebook.com/policies/cookies).

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, and you can manage ad preferences in your Meta account settings. Disabling cookies may affect how parts of the Website function.

5. Direct marketing

We do not currently send marketing emails. In future, we may send members news, updates and offers — most likely through the Circle platform. Where we do, you will be able to opt out / unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in the message or by contacting us at admin@fpsales.com.au. We will not use your personal information for marketing if you have asked us not to.

6. Disclosure of personal information

We may disclose personal information to:

  • Circle, the platform that hosts our checkout and community;
  • Meta, in connection with advertising and the Meta Pixel;
  • payment processors engaged by Circle to handle subscription payments;
  • professional advisers (such as accountants or lawyers) where reasonably necessary; and
  • government bodies or regulators where required or authorised by law.

We do not sell your personal information.

7. Overseas disclosure

Some of the third parties we use are located outside Australia. In particular, Circle and Meta are based in the United States, and your personal information may be stored or processed in the United States or other countries. By using our Website and joining the community, you acknowledge that your personal information may be disclosed to and handled by overseas recipients.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and much of the personal information involved in the community is held by Circle under its own security practices.

9. Accessing and correcting your information

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct it, by emailing admin@fpsales.com.au. We will respond within a reasonable time. Because most member information is held within Circle, you may also be able to view and update much of it directly in your Circle account settings.

10. Complaints

If you have a concern about how we have handled your personal information, please contact us at admin@fpsales.com.au and we will work to resolve it.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC):

  • Website: oaic.gov.au
  • Phone: 1300 363 992

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on our Website, and the "Last updated" date above shows when it was most recently changed.
